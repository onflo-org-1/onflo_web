Our Story

Built for the Ones with Bigger Dreams

Onflotech was born from a simple observation: technology has become the language of modern business, but not everyone speaks it — or can afford to learn.

Small and established businesses get left behind while larger players invest in digital infrastructure. Entrepreneurs with real ideas struggle to find a partner who takes them seriously at a fair price. And communities often never get useful tools at all, because there's no profit model behind them.

We built Onflotech to change that. We partner with businesses who need their first digital presence. We build alongside entrepreneurs from concept to launch. And because we believe technology should serve people, not just commerce — we build utility applications for communities, free of charge. This isn't charity. It's our reason for existing.

Our Work

Products We've Shipped

Real apps built for real problems. Two of which we built for the world, free of charge.

01
Android Consumer App
Scentique
"Which perfume do I wear tonight?"
The Problem

People own beautiful fragrances but wear them to the wrong occasion — a heavy oud to a casual lunch, a light citrus to a black-tie dinner. The decision is always a guess.

The Solution

A single scan tells you exactly which occasions your perfume is perfect for. No database to browse. No expertise needed. Point, scan, know.

02
Android Free · Society First
PlantLife AI
"Is my plant dying — and can I fix it at home?"
The Problem

Millions of households own plants they can't identify or diagnose. By the time they notice something is wrong, it's usually too late. Expert advice is expensive and inaccessible.

The Solution

Point your phone at any plant to instantly identify it, detect disease, and receive home remedy suggestions — no gardening knowledge required. Built free, for everyone, always.
